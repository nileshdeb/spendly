Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes expense entries, category names, amounts, dates, and any descriptions you add. We do not collect or store sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers, bank account details, or passwords.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of providing our expense tracking services. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and reports, and enabling you to analyze your spending patterns. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. You can delete your data at any time by deleting your account or individual expense entries.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers have access to your data only for the purpose of providing these services and are obligated to maintain confidentiality. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session while using the application. We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, but this data is not linked to your personal information or expense entries.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@spendly.example.com.
